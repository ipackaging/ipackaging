About us

Innovative Overseas almost being a pioneer in non woven products was established almost a decade ago with the goal to produce quality products for our customers. Our company is one of the renowned manufacturers and suppliers of eco-friendly custom Non Woven Bags.

Who We Are

We are the fastest emerging company in India with over 10 years of experience in the fabric industry; we are specialized in producing variety of re-usable fabric products. We have a well equipped infrastructure, we provide flawless products with rapid production rate. In addition, we have well structured testing labs with state of the art equipment that allows for testing our products and ensure quality. Our team of dedicated professionals have years of expertise in producing high quality non-woven fabric products. We work separately for every client according to their individual requirements.

Our Vision

Our vision and mission is to effectively follow our objectives and principles in producing efficient and cost-effective bags. Our main goal is to produce eco-friendly reusable bags to our esteemed customers in making the world go greener!
